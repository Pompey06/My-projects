Traffic Monitoring

Vision AI detects and identifies vehicles on the road, analyzing traffic flow and spotting violations or accidents in real-time

Public Safety

Vision AI recognizes potentially dangerous situations, such as individuals carrying weapons or acting suspiciously in public spaces, providing real-time alerts to ensure safety.

Retail Analytics

Vision AI tracks customer movements in stores, identifies product interactions, and helps optimize layouts to enhance shopping experiences and boost sales.
